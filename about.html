Who We Are

Insight Hive is a student-led initiative dedicated to bridging the digital divide. We understand that many students struggle to afford laptops upfront, which creates barriers to completing assignments, research, and online learning. Our mission is to make laptops accessible through flexible hire-purchase plans.

Our Solution

With our rent-to-own platform, students can get a laptop immediately after paying a deposit and then complete payments over six months. We provide transparency through signed agreements, automatic payment processing, and receipts for every transaction.
